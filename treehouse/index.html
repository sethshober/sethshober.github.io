Why I Belong

Work should be an extension of your life, not the other way around. If you are doing something you enjoy and are passionate about, it will shine through. I am passionate about learning and sharing that passion with others. While Treehouse provides a brand and service I certainly tout, it has appealed to me as a place to work because it has created a rich culture that empowers employees to do what they do best; a culture that is team focused, in a family-like way. The team at Treehouse is creating something I believe in, and that makes all the difference. I am extremely passionate about the things I do, and use discipline and desire to achieve whatever it is I am working towards. At the end of the day I want to have done something that mattered. Being a student of Treehouse I know the true value of your service, and after meeting members of the team and fellow students I knew it would be a fulfilling place for me. Treehouse has a lot to offer, and I believe I have a lot to offer Treehouse. Our shared beliefs and values, together, will help us change lives.
