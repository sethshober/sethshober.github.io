READ MY COVER LETTER

Greetings!

I’m looking to join a team that shares my passion to solve problems and build things. I love to learn and am pretty confident with my focus and discipline I can learn anything. I’ve been spending extensive amounts of time teaching myself web design and development.

I began with foundations in HTML5/CSS3 building responsive static pages. Starting to focus on the UI/UX of things I found that a proper workflow led to a better result. I started beginning my projects with a wireframe or mockup, mostly in Balsamiq or Sketch, so when I opened up Sublime I knew exactly where to begin. My pages needed to be interactive and dynamic, though, so JavaScript was going to be necessary. Then came jQuery for quicker prototyping and power, along with AJAX calls to APIs to bring things to life. At this point I was able to build web apps that solved real world problems, but I needed a place to share my work and collaborate with my teammates. So, of course I learned the value of version control and GitHub, as well as the command line.

This was my path to learning web development fundamentals, but my interest doesn’t stop there. I am interested in full stack JavaScript, specifically the MEAN stack. This is the direction I am looking to go. Taking it to this next level has been my current focus in building web and hybrid apps. I want to build things that people actually use, and at the end of the day I want to feel I did something that mattered.

You are looking at a unicorn here, folks. I’m somebody who can design and build something that looks and feels right, who also understands the logic behind it. I can contribute to any team, including the business side, having experience trying to start my own company.

Pair all this with the fact that I am an Appalachian Trail thru-hiker and Eagle Scout, with some pretty gnarly and invaluable life experiences, and we might just have a fit.

Let’s talk soon and see where it leads.


Cheers!
