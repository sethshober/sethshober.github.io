Why I Belong

Work should be an extension of your life, not the other way around. If you are doing something you enjoy and are passionate about, it will shine through. I am a passionate traveler and love sharing that passion with others. While Airbnb is an experience I certainly tout, it has appealed to me as a place to work because you have created a rich culture that empowers employees to do what they do best; a culture that is team focused, in a family-like way. Airbnb is something I believe in, and that makes all the difference. I am extremely passionate about the things I do, and use discipline and desire to achieve whatever it is I am working towards. At the end of the day I want to have done something that mattered. When Airbnb re-evaluated its mission and launched Bèlo, I knew it would be a fulfilling place for me. Airbnb has a lot to offer, and I believe I have a lot to offer Airbnb. Our shared beliefs and values, together, will help us create belonging.

Let's help people feel a sense of belonging.
