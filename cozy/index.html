Why I Belong

Work should be an extension of your life, not the other way around. If you are doing something you are passionate about, the work will shine through. I am passionate about helping people achieve their goals. I believe everybody has the mental capacity to do what they truly want, and we need to enable as many people as possible.

Culture is incredibly important to me, and it appears you have created a rich culture that empowers employees to do what they do best and be their unique selves. The mission of Cozy is something I believe in, and when it comes to the work you do day by day, that makes all the difference.

When it comes to my aspirations, be they personal or professional, I hone-in. It's a balancing act of discipline and desire. At the end of the day, I want to have done something that mattered, and I believe Cozy could be that fulfilling place for me. Cozy has a lot to offer, and I believe I have a lot to offer Cozy. Together, our shared beliefs and values will help everybody become a contributor.
